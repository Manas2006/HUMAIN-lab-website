# Summer Internship Program Applications Open

We're now accepting applications for our summer internship program! This is a great opportunity for undergraduate and graduate students to gain hands-on research experience.

## Program Details

- 10-week program running from June to August
- Work on cutting-edge research projects
- Mentorship from faculty and PhD students
- Stipend and housing support available

## How to Apply

Applications are due by May 1st. Visit our contact page to learn more and submit your application.

## What to Expect

Interns will work closely with our research team on projects related to human-AI interaction, ethical AI, and AI systems. You'll gain valuable experience in research methodology, data analysis, and scientific writing.
