# Lab Receives Grant for AI Ethics Research

We're excited to announce that HUMAIN Lab has been awarded a $500,000 grant from the National Science Foundation to support our research on ethical AI frameworks.

## About the Grant

This three-year grant will fund research on developing comprehensive ethical frameworks for AI development and deployment. The project will involve collaboration with industry partners and policy makers.

## Research Goals

- Develop practical frameworks for ethical AI development
- Create tools for bias detection and mitigation
- Establish best practices for responsible AI deployment

## Impact

This funding will allow us to expand our research team and accelerate our work on ensuring AI systems are developed and deployed responsibly.
