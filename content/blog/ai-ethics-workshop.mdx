# Lab Hosts AI Ethics Workshop

Last month, we hosted a successful workshop on AI ethics that brought together over 50 participants from academia, industry, and government.

## Workshop Highlights

The workshop featured keynote talks from leading researchers in AI ethics, panel discussions on governance and regulation, and hands-on sessions on bias detection and mitigation.

## Key Takeaways

- The importance of interdisciplinary collaboration in AI ethics
- The need for practical tools and frameworks for responsible AI development
- The role of policy and regulation in ensuring ethical AI deployment

## Looking Forward

We're planning to make this an annual event and are already working on next year's program. Stay tuned for updates!
