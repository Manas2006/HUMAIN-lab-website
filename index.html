Research Areas

We conduct cutting-edge research across multiple domains of artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction.

Machine Learning

Developing advanced algorithms and models that learn from data to solve complex real-world problems with improved accuracy and efficiency.

Human-Computer Interaction

Designing intuitive interfaces and interaction paradigms that make technology more accessible and user-friendly for diverse populations.

Ethical AI

Ensuring artificial intelligence systems are fair, transparent, and aligned with human values through rigorous ethical frameworks and practices.
